Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

KymeraAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information in connection with our lead intake and notification automation services.

1. Information We Collect

When a customer submits a service request through an intake form powered by KymeraAI, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service location or address
  • Description of the service issue
  • Urgency level as described by the customer
  • Timestamp of submission

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected through service request forms is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To notify the service provider (business owner) of the incoming request
  • To send the customer an automated confirmation message acknowledging receipt of their request
  • To log the request in a secure lead management dashboard accessible only by the service provider
  • To classify the priority level of the request (Emergency, Urgent, or Standard)

We do not use customer information for marketing purposes, and we do not send recurring messages beyond the single confirmation message triggered by form submission.

3. SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number on a service request form, you consent to receive a one-time automated SMS confirmation message from the service provider regarding your request. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages, reply STOP to any message you receive. For help, reply HELP.

Message frequency: one message per service request submitted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Information is shared only with:

  • The service provider (business) to whom the request was submitted
  • Infrastructure providers used to deliver our services (including Google, Twilio, and Zapier), under their respective privacy policies

5. Data Retention

Customer information submitted through intake forms is retained in the service provider's Google Sheets dashboard. Customers may request deletion of their information by contacting us at the email below.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect personal information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access-controlled data storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
