The Process

Set up once.

Custom Intake Form
We build a branded service request form for your business. Customers fill it out from your website, Google listing, or a direct link.
02
AI Priority Detection
The moment a form is submitted, our system automatically classifies it as Emergency, Urgent, or Standard based on the customer's description.

You receive an immediate SMS and email with all the lead details — customer name, issue, location, and priority level. No delay, no missed calls.
